Novalink Technologies Ltd — Company Profile
1. Company Overview
Novalink Technologies Ltd is a Financial Technology (Fintech) company headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria. Founded in 2019 to bridge the financial inclusion gap across Africa, Novalink provides digital payment solutions and mobile banking services to individual consumers and small businesses across West and East Africa.

Total Employees: 150

8. Current Security Maturity
Novalink is at a Low security maturity level. The company is establishing its GRC program for the first time. No formal security policies, risk registers or technical controls are currently documented. Security decisions are reactive rather than proactive. This engagement represents the foundation of Novalinks security governance program.
9. Reason for GRC Engagement
Novalink does not currently have a dedicated GRC function. The company has engaged an external GRC Consulting Firm to design and implement a security governance framework from the ground up. This includes building governance structures, policies, risk frameworks and technically validating controls across the organization.
10. Purpose of This Document
This company profile serves as the foundational document that informs all governance, risk and compliance decisions made throughout this engagement. All policies, frameworks, org structures and technical validations are designed specifically for Novalinks operational context, size, regulatory environment and risk appetite.
