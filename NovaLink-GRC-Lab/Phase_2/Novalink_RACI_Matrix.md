## Purpose
This RACI Matrix defines the roles and responsibilities of all
stakeholders across key security activities at Novalink Technologies
Ltd. It ensures accountability, eliminates confusion and establishes
clear ownership of every security function within the organization.

---

## RACI Key
- R = Responsible (performs the work)
- A = Accountable (owns the outcome and answers for it)
- C = Consulted (provides input before decisions are made)
- I = Informed (kept updated on progress and outcomes)

## Roles and Responsibilities Summary

### Board of Directors
- Maintains ultimate accountability for Novalinks security posture
- Approves security strategy and risk appetite
- Reviews audit findings and major incident reports

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- Sets the tone for security culture across the organization
- Accountable for business continuity and regulatory compliance
- Informed of all significant security activities and incidents

### Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
- Owns the entire information security program
- Accountable for policy development, risk management and incident
  response
- Reports security posture directly to the CEO and Board

### GRC Manager
- Responsible for developing and maintaining security policies
- Leads risk assessments and compliance monitoring activities
- Coordinates third party vendor assessments
- Reports to the CISO on all GRC matters

### GRC Analyst
- Performs day to day risk assessment and policy development tasks
- Monitors compliance with internal policies and external regulations
- Supports third party vendor assessment processes

### SOC Manager
- Responsible for incident response and vulnerability management
- Oversees the Security Operations Centre
- Coordinates with IT Manager on technical security controls

### SOC Analyst
- Performs hands on incident detection and response activities
- Conducts vulnerability scans and monitors security alerts
- Escalates incidents to the SOC Manager

### IT Manager
- Responsible for access control and vulnerability management
- Manages technical infrastructure and system security
- Supports business continuity planning from a technical perspective

### IT Support
- Implements access control changes on systems
- Provides technical support during incident response
- Maintains system configurations in line with security policies

### Legal and Compliance
- Consulted on all regulatory compliance matters
- Reviews security policies for legal and regulatory alignment
- Ensures Novalink meets CBN, NDPR and GDPR requirements

### Human Resources
- Responsible for delivering security awareness training to staff
- Manages onboarding and offboarding security procedures
- Consulted on access control and insider threat matters

### Internal Audit
- Independently reviews and validates the GRC program
- Reports audit findings directly to the Board of Directors
- Provides assurance that security controls are operating effectively

### External Auditors
- Provide independent third party assurance on security controls
- Informed of security policy and compliance status
- Support regulatory reporting requirements

## Notes
1. A dash (-) indicates no involvement in that activity
2. Each activity must have exactly one Accountable (A) owner
3. This matrix must be reviewed annually or after any major
   organizational change
4. All role holders must be trained on their RACI responsibilities
