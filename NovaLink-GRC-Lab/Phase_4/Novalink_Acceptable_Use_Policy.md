## 1. Purpose
This Acceptable Use Policy defines the rules and responsibilities
governing the use of Novalink Technologies Ltd information systems,
devices, networks and data. It ensures that all users understand
their obligations and use Novalink resources in a secure, lawful
and professional manner.

---

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All Novalink employees, contractors and third party vendors
- All devices owned or managed by Novalink
- All personal devices used to access Novalink systems (BYOD)
- All Novalink offices in Nigeria, Ghana and Kenya
- All remote and hybrid working arrangements

---

## 3. Policy Statement

### 3.1 Acceptable Use of Systems
All users must:
- Use Novalink systems only for legitimate business purposes
- Protect their login credentials and never share passwords
- Lock their screens when leaving their workstations unattended
- Report any suspicious activity or security incidents immediately
- Keep their devices and software up to date with security patches
- Use only approved software and applications on Novalink devices

### 3.2 Internet and Email Use
- Internet access is provided for business purposes
- Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere
  with work responsibilities or violate this policy
- Users must not visit websites that are illegal, offensive or
  inappropriate in a professional environment
- Emails must be professional and must not contain confidential
  information unless properly encrypted
- Users must not click on suspicious links or open unexpected
  email attachments without verification

### 3.3 Social Media Use
- Employees must not share confidential Novalink information
  on any social media platform
- Employees must not make statements that could damage Novalinks
  reputation or mislead customers
- Personal social media use on company devices during work hours
  is discouraged

### 3.4 Data Handling
- Confidential data must never be stored on personal devices
  without explicit approval from the IT Manager
- Customer PII and financial data must only be accessed on
  approved Novalink systems
- Data must not be transferred to unauthorized external storage
  devices such as USB drives
- All data sharing with third parties must be approved by the
  GRC Manager and Legal and Compliance team

### 3.5 Remote Working
- Employees working remotely must use the Novalink approved
  Virtual Private Network (VPN) at all times
- Remote workers must ensure their home networks are secured
  with strong passwords
- Confidential information must not be discussed in public places
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT Support immediately

### 3.6 Prohibited Activities
The following activities are strictly prohibited on Novalink systems:

- Unauthorized access to systems, data or networks
- Installing unauthorized software or applications
- Sharing login credentials with any other person
- Attempting to bypass or disable security controls
- Downloading or distributing illegal or inappropriate content
- Using Novalink systems for personal financial gain
- Accessing competitor systems or confidential information
- Any activity that violates Nigerian, Ghanaian or Kenyan law

---

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| CISO | Own and enforce this policy across the organization |
| GRC Manager | Monitor compliance and report violations |
| IT Manager | Implement technical controls to enforce this policy |
| HR Manager | Communicate policy to all staff during onboarding |
| All Employees | Read, understand and comply with this policy |
| SOC Analyst | Monitor systems for policy violations |

---

## 5. Compliance and Enforcement
- All employees must sign an acknowledgment form confirming they
  have read and understood this policy upon joining Novalink
- Violations of this policy will be investigated by the GRC Manager
  and HR Manager
- Disciplinary action for violations may include formal warning,
  suspension or termination depending on severity
- Serious violations involving illegal activity will be reported
  to relevant law enforcement authorities
- All violations will be documented and retained for audit purposes

---

## 6. Review and Update Schedule
- This policy will be reviewed annually by the GRC Manager and CISO
- The policy will also be reviewed following any major security
  incident or significant organizational change
- All updates must be approved by the CISO and communicated
  to all staff
